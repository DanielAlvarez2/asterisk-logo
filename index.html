Asterisk Software


We specialize in creating software for the restaurant industry by updgrading websites to add more functionality. The goal is to seamlessly merge digital online menus with physical, printed, table-top paper menus. Our solution allows for both to be simultaneously updated with one simple, intuitive user-interface. Restaurant owners and managers can easily control content for all their venue's menus directly from their phone.




About Us


We bring decades of hospitality experience from Manhattan, Las Vegas, and L.A. With an insider's view of the inner workings of the service industry, we are able to produce software that provides a comprehensive, detail-oriented solution to some of the most challenging pain-points of menu content management. We aim to serve you with the same level of service that you provide to your guests.




Pricing



Several factors will affect the pricing of your project: number of menus, number of total pages, complexity of your existing website, etc., but as a broad base-line you can expect a quote of $1000 minimum.

Frequently Asked Questions



Q: Are you a website design agency?
A: NO! While website "design" is well within our capabilities, it is not our main focus. We provide a more data-centric software solution that uses your existing website as a display output for the ever-changing content of your venue's daily offerings.


Q: Do you charge a monthly subscription fee?
A: No. You pay once, upon delivery of the final product. There are no subscription fees.


Q: How long does it take to receive the finished product?
A: You can generally expect a project to last about 2 months to complete.
