Many of today's restaurants are struggling with the same dilemma: Physical Menus VS Digital Menus; traditional VS modern. Each choice comes with its own set of pros and cons.

Traditional physical paper menus are what older clientele have become accustomed to holding in their hands. Some of these guests have gone so far as to post on social media platforms that they would refuse to return to any establishment that does not provide a physical menu.

On the other side of the debate is the option to use purely digital menus: for example, table tents with QR Codes that guests can scan with their cell phone to view the restaurant's online menus. The tech-savvy younger generations are quick to adopt this option.

So it seems like either choice would attract more of one demographic versus the other.

Not to mention the quagmire that results from choosing BOTH options and trying to keep your digital menus up to date with your printed menus.

So what's the solution?...

We can make it so you can have the best of both worlds! Traditional paper menus AND digital online menus, both kept completely in sync at all times without any extra effort.

Our solution takes your current, existing website, and just gives it extra functionality. You can update both your digital and paper menus simultaneously. You make 1 change, and both menus are seamlessly up to date. With our software, you print your paper menus directly from your website, ensuring the two documents never differ from each other.

Pricing



Several factors will affect the pricing of your project: number of menus, number of total pages, complexity of your existing website, etc., but as a broad base-line you can expect a quote of $1000 minimum.
